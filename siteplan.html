Vida Sana U (Healthy Life U)

This name represents a hub for university students to access quick, actionable health tips tailored to their busy schedules.

Optional domain: vidasana-u.com (available for purchase).

Site Purpose

This website will provide university students with:

  • Concise advice on nutrition, exercise, and sleep.
  • Interactive features (random tips, saving favorites via JavaScript).
  • A contact form to submit questions or feedback.

Scenarios

  • "What are quick, healthy meals I can prepare in a dorm room?"
  • "How can I improve my sleep schedule during exams?"

Color Schema

  • #8cc63f (SeaGreen): Headers, buttons, accents.
  • #FFFFFF (White): Background.
  • #333333 (Dark Gray): Body text.

Typography

  • Font 1: 'Open Sans' (Google Fonts) - Body text.
  • Font 2: 'Montserrat' (Google Fonts) - Headings.
